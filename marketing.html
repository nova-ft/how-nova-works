The agents behind the story

Four specialised agents split the work of top-of-funnel marketing. Tap any card to open it. Each carries an autonomy tier — how far it can act on its own — and a build wave — when it comes online in the rollout.

ACROSS NOVA

How it connects

Marketing doesn't work in isolation. It is the top of a company-wide funnel — feeding other departments, sharing its output, and always keeping a human on the publish button.

Feeds Sales

Demand-Gen turns campaigns and landing pages into warm, qualified leads. Those leads flow straight into Sales as the handoff at the bottom of the marketing funnel.

Marketing → Sales · leads

Feeds Strategy

The Market-Intel Agent's read on regions and competitors doesn't stop at Marketing — the same signal feeds Strategy, so company direction is grounded in live market intelligence.

Marketing → Strategy · intel

Shared assets

The Brand / Design Agent's decks, one-pagers and templates aren't single-use. They're reused across the whole company, so every team shows up on-brand without rebuilding the basics.

On-brand · reused company-wide

Agents propose, humans approve

Every Marketing agent runs at Tier 2 or below — they draft, design and assemble, but they never publish on their own. A human approves every publish. That's how Nova moves at machine speed while keeping the voice, the brand and the accountability firmly human.
