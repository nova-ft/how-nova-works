Operating principle

Agents propose, humans approve

The L1 Deflect Agent can act — it's Tier 3 and works from an allowlist of safe, reversible responses. But anything that touches a bank's live system is a real fix, and a real fix always follows the SDLC: proposed by an agent, reviewed and approved by a human, shipped through Engineering.

The rule of thumb: deflect and diagnose autonomously; change nothing in production without a human on the approval.
Two destinations: incidents flow down to Engineering; health flows across to Cortex for Customers & Finance.
